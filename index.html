FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How much does a website cost at Skillence?
+
Our websites start at just ₹2,999 for a portfolio website. Business websites start at ₹9,999 and Shopify stores start at ₹5,999. We also offer EMI payment options with zero hidden charges. The final price depends on your specific requirements — WhatsApp us for a free custom quote.
How long does it take to build a website?
+
Portfolio websites are typically delivered in 5–7 days. Business websites take 7–10 days. Shopify stores take 7–14 days depending on the number of products. We have a 7-day delivery guarantee on standard projects — if we miss the deadline, you get a full refund.
Will my website rank on Google?
+
Yes! Every website we build includes basic on-page SEO — proper meta tags, schema markup, fast load speed, and Google-friendly URL structure. Business plan clients get advanced SEO including local SEO setup, Google Search Console, and keyword-optimised content. Results typically appear within 30–90 days.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
+
Absolutely. Every single website we build is 100% mobile-responsive and tested across all screen sizes — from small phones to large desktops. Mobile-first design is the standard for all our projects because 80%+ of Indian internet users browse on mobile devices.
What information do you need from me to start?
+
To get started, we need your business name, what you do, your target audience, your logo (if you have one), any photos or content you want on the site, and your color preferences. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we'll guide you through the whole process step by step on WhatsApp.
Do you provide hosting and domain as well?
+
We provide complete guidance on setting up domain and hosting at the best prices (typically ₹500–1,500/year for domain + hosting combined). We handle the entire technical setup and deployment for you. Alternatively, we can deploy on your existing hosting at no extra cost.
Can I make changes to my website after it's delivered?
+
Yes! All our plans include free support for 1–3 months after delivery during which we handle minor updates at no charge. After the support period, we offer affordable maintenance packages. Major redesigns are quoted separately based on requirements.
Do you serve clients outside Delhi?
+
Yes! We serve clients across all of India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Lucknow, and every other city. 100% of our work is done remotely over WhatsApp and email, so location is never a barrier.
